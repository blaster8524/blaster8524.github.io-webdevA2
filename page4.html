Trending Soulslike Games

With all that is said, here is a list for trending Soulslike Games that leave positive reviews for new players and veterans alike, though most of these titles are AAA titles because no doubt, the company that makes these games were the ones who were making the Souls series after all.

Top of the List: Elden Ring

Elden Ring is a 2022 action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki and co-written by fantasy writer George R. R. Martin, who provided material for the setting. The legendary writer himself of Game of Thrones to make the narrative for Elden Ring, along with FromSoftware wanting to create an open-world game with gameplay similar to their existing Souls series, intending Elden Ring to act as an evolution of Dark Souls makes a groundbreaking sequel to the Souls Series, definitely worth trying out and earns the top of the list.

Runner up: Sekiro

Gameplay is focused on stealth, exploration, and combat, with a particular emphasis on boss battles. The game takes place in a fictionalized Japan during the Sengoku period and makes strong references to Buddhist mythology and philosophy. A different take of the Souls series formula FromSoftware uses, it gives a refreshing and different feel, with a different mechanic in defeating enemies, depleting enemies' posture rather than whittling their health away. Sekiro takes the Runner up with its unique shinobi feel and japanese aesthetics
